Welcome to Jogalfy, your personal music streaming service powered by Navidrome. This guide will help you get started.

## What is Jogalfy?

Jogalfy gives you access to a private music library. You can listen to music from your browser or your phone.

## Accessing Jogalfy

You can access Jogalfy by visiting:

[https://music.pattyjog.al](https://music.pattyjog.al)

## Logging In

You will need a username and password to log in. If you don't have one, please contact the administrator.

## Using the Web Player

The web player is easy to use. You can browse your music by artist, album, or song. You can also create playlists.

## Mobile Apps

You can use any <a href="https://www.navidrome.org/docs/apps/" target="_blank">Subsonic-compatible app</a> to connect to Jogalfy on your mobile device. When configuring the app, use the following details:

-   **Server Address:** `https://music.pattyjog.al`
-   **Username:** Your Jogalfy username
-   **Password:** Your Jogalfy password

Some popular apps include:

-   **iOS:** substreamer, play:Sub
-   **Android:** DSub, Subtracks

## Questions?

If you have any questions, please reach out to the administrator.
