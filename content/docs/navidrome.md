## Overview

Navidrome is a self-hosted, open source music server and streamer. It gives you the freedom to listen to your music collection from any device, anywhere.

Our instance is hosted at: [https://music.pattyjog.al](https://music.pattyjog.al)

## Getting an Account

To get an account on this Navidrome instance, please contact the administrator. Currently, public registration is disabled to ensure quality of service and security.

## Recommended Clients

Navidrome is compatible with all Subsonic-compatible clients. Here are some recommended ones:

### Mobile

- **Android**: [Symfonium](https://www.symfonium.app/), [Dsub](https://github.com/daneren211/dsub)
- **iOS**: [Amperfy](https://apps.apple.com/app/amperfy/id1565133481), [play:Sub](https://apps.apple.com/app/playsub-music-streamer/id424518468)

### Desktop

- **Web**: Use the web interface at [music.pattyjog.al](https://music.pattyjog.al)
- **Sonixd**: [Github](https://github.com/jeffvli/sonixd) - A full-featured desktop client for Subsonic and Jellyfin.

## Configuration

When setting up your client, use the following details:

- **Server URL**: `https://music.pattyjog.al`
- **Username**: Your assigned username
- **Password**: Your assigned password
